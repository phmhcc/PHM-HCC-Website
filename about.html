About Our Church

Pentecostal Holiness Mission Hope Christian Church (PHM HCC) is a Bible-believing church committed to preaching the Gospel of Jesus Christ, nurturing believers, and serving the community through love, prayer, and outreach. We welcome everyone to worship with us and grow in faith.

Our Vision

To build a Christ-centered church that transforms lives through faith, love, and the power of the Holy Spirit.

Our Mission

To preach the Gospel, disciple believers, equip leaders, and reach communities with the message of hope and salvation.

Core Values

  • Faith in Jesus Christ
  • Prayer and Worship
  • Love and Compassion
  • Integrity and Holiness
  • Community Service
  • Evangelism and Discipleship
